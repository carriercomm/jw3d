Quick Tools

Quick Tools is an add-on for Blender that provides a series of macros and custom operators to enhance your workflow. It works by expediting many small tasks that become quite repetitive.

The add-on is available, free, under a GPL v2 license on Github. You are under no obligation to purchase it, but I appreciate your support. All procedes go back into developing the tools further.

Learn how to use Quick Tools via my tutorial on CG Cookie.
